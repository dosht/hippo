### S10: 5-Session Validation Gate

**As Mustafa**, I want to run the full pipeline (ingest, compact, extract) on exactly 5 hand-selected
past sessions and review the resulting gold entries before unleashing the pipeline on full history,
so that I can catch quality problems (lost near-misses, too-abstract entries, wrong duplicate
detection) at small scale first.

This story is a hard gate. S11 (full-history ingest) is blocked until this story is marked done.

#### Acceptance criteria

- [ ] 5 sessions chosen manually from ~/.claude/projects/: at least 2 from different projects, at least 1 subagent session, at least 1 session known to contain a near-miss failure
- [ ] `python scripts/ingest.py` run with those 5 sessions present; manifest shows 5 bronze entries
- [ ] `python scripts/compact.py` run; silver summaries reviewed manually and confirmed to contain the near-miss failure from the chosen session
- [ ] `python scripts/extract.py` run; resulting gold entries reviewed manually
- [ ] Human checklist completed:
  - [ ] No abstract principles without supporting concrete detail
  - [ ] Near-miss failure preserved in at least one entry
  - [ ] No entry is a direct duplicate of a hand-crafted seed entry from S1
  - [ ] Frontmatter valid (all fields present, no em dashes, confidence: medium)
- [ ] Any quality issues found are fixed in compact.py or extract.py prompts before proceeding
- [ ] `qmd query` returns the new entries correctly
- [ ] Validation findings documented in a comment in docs/developer-guide/README.md (one paragraph, not a separate file)

#### Architect Notes
